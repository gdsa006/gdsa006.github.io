About Progressive Services & Solutions (PSNS)

Located in Auckland, New Zealand, Progressive Services & Solutions (PSNS) serves as a trusted hub for international trade and logistics solutions. Our strategic location enables us to efficiently facilitate seamless cross-border transactions, connecting businesses worldwide.

Our Mission

At PSNS, our mission is to streamline global commerce by providing reliable import-export services with a strong emphasis on efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Commitment to Excellence

We are dedicated to delivering exceptional value to our clients and partners, ensuring every transaction is executed with precision and professionalism.

Our Products

Scrap Metals

We offer a comprehensive range of scrap metals, including ferrous and non-ferrous materials. Our scrap metals are sourced from reliable suppliers and are available in various quantities to meet your specific needs.

Various Metals

In addition to scrap metals, we provide various metals for industrial and commercial applications. From aluminum to copper to steel, our diverse range of metals ensures that you find the right materials for your projects.

Import Range
