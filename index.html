About Us

Located at Auckland, New Zealand, Progressive Services & Solutions (PSNS) serves as a trusted hub for international trade and logistics solutions. Our strategic location enables us to efficiently facilitate seamless cross-border transactions, connecting businesses worldwide. At PSNS, our mission is to streamline global commerce by providing reliable import-export services with a strong emphasis on efficiency and customer satisfaction.
