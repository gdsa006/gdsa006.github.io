Our Mission

Dedicated to continuing Sardarni Gurcharan Kaur's legacy of promoting education and empowerment. We strive to provide educational opportunities to underprivileged students and support their journey towards a brighter future.

Our History & Accomplishments

Sardarni Gurcharan Kaur

Our Founder's Story

Gurcharan Kaur D/o Variyam Singh was born in a very religious family. She was married to S. Gurcharan Singh. Despite having a very struggled life, both of them completed their education after marriage and adopted the profession of teaching.

Unfortunately, a little before retirement Smt. Gurcharan Kaur developed a brain tumour and was operated upon twice, lost her memory besides other disabilities and ultimately left this world in June, 1991. Her husband S. Gurcharan Singh founded this Trust in her memory on 24.10.1991.

Trust Objectives

  • Providing medical aid to deserving persons without any distinction of religion, caste or creed.
  • Provide financial aid to educational, social welfare institutions, health centres or homes for the poor.
  • Help the poor and the deserving regardless of their religion, caste and creed.
  • Award stipends and financial assistance, with preference to fatherless children or orphans.
  • Arrange treatment for the sick and grant financial assistance for clothings, food and medicines.
  • Publish books and literature to inculcate fine values of good citizenship and high moral character.
  • Help young men and women in acquiring technical know-how to earn their livelihood.
